About SSC Knockout

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a high-quality, accessible, and effective platform for students preparing for the SSC and other competitive government exams. We believe that with smart practice and consistent effort, every aspirant can achieve their goal. This platform is designed to help you practice smart, identify your weak areas, and crack your exam with confidence.

Our Team

We are a small team of passionate educators and developers based in India, dedicated to creating tools that make a real difference in students' lives. We understand the challenges of exam preparation and have built this app with the features we wished we had during our own studies.
